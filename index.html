Multiplication and Division of Fractions

Multiplication of Fractions :

For multiplication, it doesn’t matter whether the fractions are like or unlike:

  1. Multiply the numerators.
  2. Multiply the denominators.
  3. Simplify the result, if possible.

Example : 25 × 34 = 2 × 35 × 4 = 310

Division of Fractions :

To divide fractions:

  1. Keep the first fraction as it is.
  2. Flip (take the reciprocal of) the second fraction.
  3. Multiply the fractions.

Example : 58 ÷ 23

Flip 23 to 32

Multiply: 58 × 32 = 5 × 38 × 2 = 1516
